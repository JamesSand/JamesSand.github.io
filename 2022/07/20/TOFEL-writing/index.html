独立写作

开头段

三要素：

  1. 社会背景
  2. 改写题中观点
  3. 自己观点

模板1

… has long been the center of public attention.

Some people claim that …

However, from my perspective, …

模板2

将模板1的第一句话替换为

When it comes to …, opinions vary, based on people’s diverse backgrounds, experiences, and values.

模板中的替换

Some people claim that

= a large propotion of

= the vast majority of

Some people claim that

= argue / deem / reckon

= opt to do

However, from my perspective

= Nevertheless

= Nonetheless

However, from my perspective

= as far as I see

= as far as I am concerned

= from my point of view

= in my opinion

表达观点

xxx brings more merits / benefits

this view does not hold water

结尾段

模板

(1) All in all / To sum up / In conclusion / In a nutshell / On the whole, …

(2)

  • from what has been discussed above,

  • taking into account all the factors discussed above,

  • considering all the factors above,

  • from all the analysis above,

(3) it is safe / natural / reasonable to come to / draw / reach / arrive at the conclusion that + 全文总论点同义改写

段落展开

举例方法

举例技巧1 具体化 + 有效细节展开

举例技巧2 情节跌宕起伏，前后对比

一个健康的例子，可以背一下

Take my friend Jake, for an example. He used to love deep-fried food and sugar, staying at home all day without any physical activity(这里activity是活动，不可数). But when he was diagnosed with diabetes last year, he decided to make a change.

Now, apart from hitting the gym regularly and spending at least two hours there, he plays tennis every day with his wife. His weight went from 255 pounds to 180 pounds, and he has gotten rid of the disease.

个例 / 事例

For example, …

Take … as an example.

My brother is a good case in point.

This point is best illustrated by the case of …

一个电子产品的例子，可以背一下

My little sister Amy is a good case in point. She used to be a painfully shy girl with few friends, since she spent most of her time studying and had no shared moments with friends.

However, things began to change after I taught her how to play Honor of Kings, a multiplayer online battle arena video game. This game allows her to connect with people and get feedback on how well she is playing. Besides, it creates plenty of topics to share with people around her, and now every day she plays the game with her classmates and they become faithful friends.

  1. 高中生 -> 学校
  2. 工作者-> 公司 + 工作职责
  3. 大学生 -> 学校 + 专业
