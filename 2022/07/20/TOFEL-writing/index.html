独立写作

文章结构

一边倒

主体段替换表达

  • To begin with = First of all
  • In addition = On top of that
  • Lastly = Last but not the least = Furthermore

让步转折

陈述-递进-驳倒

这种结构和上一种结构的区别在于，这种结构在转折-驳倒段要说明的是对方观点的好处可仍然可以被己方观点所实现。

开头段

倒金字塔模型。从社会现象到人们的观点，再到自己的观点。

从宏观逐渐具体。

  1. 陈述现象

  2. 表现争论

  3. 给出自己的观点

话题导入

  • 描述一个现象
    • It is quite common these days for people, especially teenagers to use various electronic devices in their daily life.
    • The increasing popularity of electronic devices has become a significant feature of mordern life in many developed countries.
    • A problem of mordern education is the declining level of learning efficiency among some students.
    • It has been a common dilemma(两难境地) for people to choose between work and family.
    • There are many learning styles, and it is important that we look for classes that match our academic preferences.
    • Society generally holds the idea that people who graduate from a overseas institution are stronger job candidates in terms of(就…而言) their language abilities and expansive thinking in global context.
  • 根据时间做对比
    • In the past, the teaching and learning tools in class were restricted to the blackboard, the chalk, the textbook; these days, the Ipad, the laptop and many other electronic devices have become part of the class.

引出题目问题

  • 引发争论
    • Some people think that…, while others believe that…
    • On the one hand, … On the other hand, …
    • People have different views about whether …
  • 引出一方观点
    • Some people / educationalists / scientists … think that …
    • Many people preferr (to do) sth.
    • It is argued that …
    • Taking into account …, some people propose that …

明确观点

  • Personally, I believe that…
  • From my perspective, …
  • As for me, …
  • In my case, …
